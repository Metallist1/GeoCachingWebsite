Question 7

A man has a bus stop outside his house. One of the buses drives to the pet shop and the other one drives to the computer shop. Both buses always arrive exactly on time, but does not arrive at the same time.

The man visits a shop every day at a random time and just takes the first bus that arrives. (he does this forever)

Which one of the following cannot occur?

  1. A. He arrives more often in the pet shop than the computer shop.
  2. B. He arrives more often in the computer shop than the pet shop.
  3. C. He arrives an equal amount of times at the pet shop and computer shop.
  4. D. He never arrives at the computer store.
